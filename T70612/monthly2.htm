Monthly Cleaning and Lubrication

Clean the flash removal ball screws and relubricate them with MOBILITH SHC 100. There are two greasing points, one each for the horizontal and vertical axes.
Clean and lubricate the backstop leadscrew and shot bolts. There are five greasing points.
Beware: they use different lubricants.

Two, marked A, are for the leadscrew bearing.
Use MOBILITH SHC 100

Two, marked B, are for the shotbolts.
Use MOBILGREASE XHP 221

One, marked C, is for the leadscrew.
Use MOBILITH SHC 100
Clean and lubricate the clamp linkage. There are four greasing points under the clamp side covers, two at each side of the clamp.

Use MOBILITH SHC 100
Lubricate the roller steady. There are four greasing points on the side of the lower part of the roller steady.

Use MOBILITH SHC 100
Clean and lubricate the swarf shredder bearings. There are four greasing points, two on each side of the shredder.

Use MOBILITH SHC 100
Clean the tie bar slots and the backstop rack and pinion and lubricate them with the recommended dry lube spray.

Use ROCOL DRY MOLY SPRAY or equivalent.
Clean around the forge cylinder knuckle and lubricate with MOBILGREASE XHP 321 MINE.

Note:

Oils and lubricants shown here are Mobil products. For equivalents from other manufacturers see here.
