Six Monthly Maintenance

Clean around the spindle lubrication oil filter and replace it.
Check the condition of the spindle drive belt and replace it if necessary. Check the belt tension using a belt frequency meter and adjust it as necessary. The correct tension for a used belt gives a frequency of 66 Hz.
Check the encoder drive belt tension using a belt frequency meter and adjust it as necessary. The correct tension for a used belt gives a frequency of 150 Hz.
Check the security of all fasteners. Pay particular attention to those for
  • chuck
  • clamp
  • spindle drive motor
  • brake calipers
Bleed the brake calipers.
