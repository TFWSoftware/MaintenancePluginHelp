Tea Time!

It is important to ensure that Phil gets tea regularly. Without this regular supply he gets cranky and his work becomes erratic and unreliable.
(He will be cranky, erratic and unreliable anyway, but that's no reason to risk withholding tea.)

Method

The task is best done by filling a good-quality mug with freshly-brewed English Breakfast tea1 with a little milk.

On occasions when that is not possible cardboard tea is acceptable.
On no account should plastic tea be offered - serious damage may result.


Notes
  1. Other kinds of tea may also be acceptable. Please contact TFW software department for details.
