Six Monthly Cleaning and Lubrication

In addition to the daily, monthly and three-monthly schedules:

Clean around the spindle lubrication oil filter and replace it.
Clean around the motor bearing grease nipple and lubricate with GADUS S3V100 2.
The bearing at the drive end of the motor is accessible when the pulley cover is removed. The bearing at the other end of the motor does not need to be lubricated.
Lubricate the flash removal linear bearings with GADUS S3V100 2. There are eight greasing points, a group of four for each axis.
Clean around the backstop and roller steady linear bearings and lubricate with GADUS S3V100 2. There is one greasing point on each of the four bearings.
Clean around the swarf conveyor bearings and apply GADUS S3V100 2.
Clean around the filler and check the level in the swarf shredder gearbox. Top up if necessary with OMALA S2G100.
Check the spindle drive belt for wear or damage. Replace the belt if necessary. Check and adjust the belt tension.
Check the security of all fasteners, particularly those for the chuck, clamp, spindle drive motor and brake calipers.
Bleed the brake calipers.
