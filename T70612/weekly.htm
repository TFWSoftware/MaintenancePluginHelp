Weekly Checks

In addition to the daily schedule:

Check the security of the chuck and clamp jaw tooling.
Drain the waste oil container. Investigate the source of any significant amount of oil and rectify any leaks. Dispose of waste oil in an environmentally sensitive way.
The drain point is at the head end of the machine bed.
