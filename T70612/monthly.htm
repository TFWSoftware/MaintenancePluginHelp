Monthly Cleaning, Lubrication and Checks

In addition to the daily schedule:

Clean the flash removal ball screws and relubricate them with GADUS S3V100 2. There are two greasing points, one each for the horizontal and vertical axes.
Clean and lubricate the backstop leadscrew and shot bolts. There are five greasing points.
Beware: they use different lubricants.

Two, marked A, are for the leadscrew bearing. Use GADUS S3V100 2
Two, marked B, are for the shotbolts. Use GADUS S2V220 1
One, marked C, is for the leadscrew. Use GADUS S3V100 2
Clean and lubricate the clamp linkage. There are four greasing points under the clamp side covers, two at each side of the clamp.

Use GADUS S3V100 2
Lubricate the roller steady. There are four greasing points on the side of the lower part of the roller steady.

Use GADUS S3V100 2
Clean and lubricate the swarf shredder bearings. There are four greasing points, two on each side of the shredder.

Use GADUS S3V100 2
Clean the tie bar slots and the backstop rack and pinion and lubricate them with the recommended dry lube spray.

Use ROCOL DRY MOLY SPRAY or equivalent.
Clean around the forge cylinder knuckle and lubricate with GADUS S2V220 AD2.
Check the condition of the spindle brake pads and replace them if the thickness is less than 6mm.

Note:

Oils and lubricants shown here are Shell products. For equivalents from other manufacturers see here.
