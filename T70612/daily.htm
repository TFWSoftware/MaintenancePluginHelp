Daily Cleaning and Checks

Clean down the machine, removing swarf and debris from the machine bed and the clamp.
Clean the faces of the clamp component sensor with a clean soft cloth. There are two parts to the sensor, one each side of the clamp.
Inspect the machine for signs of leaking hydraulic joints or leaking heat-exchanger joints. Report or rectify any such leaks.
Check the functioning of safety equipment and ensure that any failure is rectified before the machine is operated.
Check the fluid levels in the hydraulic pack, head and slide lubrication reservoirs. Top up as required.

Hydraulic oil is TELLUS OIL S2M 32 or 46.
Spindle lubrication oil is OMALA S2G100.
Slide lubrication grease is GADUS S2V220 1.

Note:

Oils and lubricants shown here are Shell products. For equivalents from other manufacturers see here.
