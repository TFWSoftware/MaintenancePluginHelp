Monthly Cleaning and Checks

Check the condition of all the slideway wipers. Clean or replace them if necessary.
There are wipers
  • on the front of the head beneath the chuck
  • on the vertical and horizontal flash removal bearings
  • on the backstop carriage linear bearings
  • on the roller steady carriage linear bearings
  • on the clamp base jaws
Check the condition of the roller steady rollers. Clean or replace them if necessary.
Check the condition of the spindle brake pads and replace them if the thickness is less than 6mm.
Clean the backstop screw gaiter and check its condition. Replace it if necessary.
