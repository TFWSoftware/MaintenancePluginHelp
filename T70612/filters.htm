Filter Cleaning

Clean or replace the spindle blower motor filter.
The manufacturer recommends that it can be cleaned and reused 2 or 3 times. Cleaning can be undertaken by shaking or by using a vacuum cleaner or a clean airline. Take care not to damage the filter medium.
