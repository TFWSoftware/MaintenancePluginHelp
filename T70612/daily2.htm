Daily Lubrication and Checks

Check the fluid levels in the hydraulic pack reservoir. Top up as required.

Hydraulic oil is MOBIL DTE 24 or 25.
Check the fluid levels in the head and slide lubrication reservoirs. Top up as required.

Spindle lubrication oil is MOBILGEAR 600 XP-100.
Slide lubrication grease is MOBILGREASE XHP 221.

Note:

Oils and lubricants shown here are Mobil products. For equivalents from other manufacturers see here.
