Yearly Cleaning, Lubrication and Checks

In addition to the daily, monthly, three-monthly, and six-monthly schedules:

Clean and lubricate the operator door guide bearings. There are two lubrication points on each door, accessible through holes in the top frame.

Use GADUS S3V100 2.
Inspect all flexible hoses for damage and repair or replace as necessary.
Inspect the spindle brake disc for wear or damage. The maximum runout allowed is 0.15mm. The disc may be skimmed to rectify runout subject to a minimum thickness of 11mm. Replace the disc if necessary.
