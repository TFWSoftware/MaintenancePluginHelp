Six Monthly Cleaning and Lubrication

Clean around the motor bearing grease nipple and lubricate with MOBILITH SHC 100.
The bearing at the drive end of the motor is accessible when the pulley cover is removed. The bearing at the other end of the motor does not need to be lubricated.
Lubricate the flash removal linear bearings with MOBILITH SHC 100. There are eight greasing points, a group of four for each axis.
Clean around the backstop and roller steady linear bearings and lubricate with MOBILITH SHC 100. There is one greasing point on each of the four bearings.
Clean around the swarf conveyor bearings and apply MOBILITH SHC 100.
Clean around the filler and check the level in the swarf shredder gearbox. Top up if necessary with MOBILGEAR 600 XP-100.

Note:
